Simple Omelette Recipe

an easy quick,perfect for any meal. this classic omelette combines beaten eeges cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables,or meats

preparation time
  • Total: approximately 10 minute
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredient

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings:cheese,dices vegetable, cooked meat , herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:

    In a blow,bit the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed, You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.

  2. Heat the pan:

    place a non-stickfrying pan in over medium well and add butter or oil

  3. Cook teh omelette:

    Once butter is emlted and bublling, pour in the egges. Tile the pan to ensure the egges evenly coat the surface

  4. Add fillings:

    When the eggs begin to set at edges but still slightly runny in the middle, spring your chosen fillings over on the half oh omelette

  5. Fold and serve:

    As the omlette continues to cook, carefully lift on edge and fold it over the fillings.Let it cooked for another minute, then slid it on to a plate.

  6. Enjoy:

    Serve hot, Enjoy it
